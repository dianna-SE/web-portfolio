ABOUT ME

DIANNA is an Asian-American singer & songwriter. She first started recording music covers before immersing into songwriting. Featuring in many substantial labels including Suit of Bullets, Frequency Music, and Heaven Sent by SLANDER and has reached over a million Spotify streams and YouTube plays worldwide, she aims to inspire talented individuals through creativity and originality through music.
